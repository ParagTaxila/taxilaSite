Course Description

This novel 1-year online PGPM with Business Analytics program from Taxila Business School is designed to prepare the next generation of managers and business executives. The specializations are designed and delivered by top faculty members and industry experts through regularly recorded lectures.

  • Gain a diverse range of essential management skills with experiential projects
  • Understand business domains tactically and strategically
  • Develop problem-solving and critical thinking skills
  • Use analytics tools to drive better business decisions
  • Build a strong business network and peer group support
  • Enhance your financial position and secure a high-paying salary

What Does This Course Offer?

The Post Graduate Program in Management (PGPM) at Taxila Business School is designed to equip professionals with the tools, knowledge, and mindset needed to excel in today’s competitive business environment. By integrating cutting-edge analytics with core business principles, this program offers a perfect blend of theoretical knowledge and practical skills.

  • A Comprehensive Learning Journey: Develop a strong foundation in business management, enhanced by advanced tools like Power BI, Python, and Tableau.
  • Flexible and Convenient Learning: Access recorded lectures from top-notch faculty, enabling you to learn at your own pace, from anywhere
  • Dual Specialization Advantage: Combine Business Analytics with one other specialization of your choice, ensuring both technical expertise and domain knowledge.
  • Personalized Learning Paths: Opt for an additional third specialization to diversify your skillset and increase your marketability.
  • Real-World Application: Engage in capstone projects, case studies, and industry-relevant simulations to translate knowledge into action.
  • Expert Faculty: Learn from experienced academicians and industry leaders across domains, providing a well-rounded perspective
  • Affordable Excellence: A competitive fee structure ensures maximum value for your investment.

Objectives of the Post Graduate Program in Management (PGPM):

  • Master Data-Driven Decision Making: Build proficiency in leveraging data analytics tools like Power BI and Python to drive informed business strategies.
  • Enhance Analytical and Critical Thinking: Cultivate the ability to analyse complex business problems and devise effective, evidence-based solutions.
  • Foster Strategic and Innovative Approaches: Equip participants with the mindset to think strategically and innovate in competitive business landscapes.
  • Specialized Expertise for Industry Readiness: Combine Business Analytics with domain specializations to ensure a blend of technical and functional knowledge.
  • Real-World Application: Gain hands-on experience through case studies and real-life examples to bridge theory with practice.
