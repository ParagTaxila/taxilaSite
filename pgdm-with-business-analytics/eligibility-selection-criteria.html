Eligibility & Selection Criteria

Taxila accepts CAT, CMAT and XAT. Aspirants seeking admission to Taxila's PGDM in Business Analytics must have a minimum of 50% marks in graduation for the General category and 45% for the reserved category, as per AICTE norms. Students appearing in their final year of graduation are also eligible to apply.

The selection process is as below:
  • 1

    Personal Interview
    Skype round - 20 Marks

  • 2

    Online case study analysis
    under controlled environment - 20 Marks

Selected candidates are required to deposit ₹50,000 as a registration fee, as mentioned in the call letter. Failure to do so will result in the cancellation of the candidature, and the seat will be offered to the next candidate on the waiting list.

If a candidate's name does not appear in the selection list, they may deposit ₹25,000 (non-refundable) to remain on the waiting list. On the cancellation/ dropout of students, the seat will be offered to such candidates on the priority/ merit basis. In case of non-selection, Rs.25000/- will be refunded back to the student on the 10th day of Batch start. In the case of selection Rs.25000/ will be adjusted against registration fee.

Suitable weightings for Work Experience, Academic & Regional background, Professional Qualification, Institution of Excellence etc. are also given.

NOTE: Applying for the admission does not ensure that candidate will be called for GD / PI process or get admission. The discretion of the admission committee of the Institute will be final in all such matters.

Decision Notification

We will post our decision on the email you have mentioned in your application form to notify you when your decision is available. We do not accommodate requests for an early decision, or for decision information to be provided via phone or in person.

If you are offered admission, we want you to make an informed decision; for this reason, we may share limited information with Taxila alumni and/or current students who may contact you to welcome you to the Taxila community and answer any questions you have.

Time of seven days would be given to the selected candidates from the day of declaration of final selection list to deposit the first installment.

Residential Facilities

Taxila offers fully air-cooled, Wi-Fi-enabled hostel facilities in Jaipur. The environment is highly conducive to academic learning. It provides comfortable living with facilities such as air-cooled hostel rooms, round-the-clock campus security, a dining hall, gym, and sports facilities. The institute has more than one location for the hostel accommodation with similar facilities. It may be provided on twin sharing or single or more sharing basis depending upon the number of students admitted. The allotment of the hostel to a student will be made at the sole discretion of the management. Take a campus tour
