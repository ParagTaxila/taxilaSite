About The Program

The entire 18 month EPGDM course is delivered through a carefully selected 20 core and elective specialization courses of 3 credits each delivered over first 5 trimesters. The final i.e. 6th trimester will have a project course equal to 6 credits. Each credit is equal to 10 hours of class engagement

The Course will be delivered in a hybrid mode where the students will have a choice to attend their classes online or offline. The usual classes will be held at weekends on Saturday - 06:00 PM to 09:00 PM and on Sunday 10:00 AM to 01:00 PM.

Continuing Leadership Coaching

Tailored leadership coaching is an integral component of the EPGDM, honing leadership and management skills and helping students to become aware and intentionally modify their individual management styles. The coaching module runs throughout the entire EPGDM programme and the participants continue to get steady advice and backing from the faculty, mentors & coaches. The process involves analysis of your skills and potential and drawing up of an action plan with personal development goals that you can execute at your work. This module also includes group training courses that help you enhance your team leadership strengths while building a clear personal awareness of areas still in need of additional growth.

Why an executive PGDM is important for your career

Evolving a professional network, increasing key knowledge and skills and hastening career progression are three big reasons why aspiring professionals choose to go to business schools.

However, time being such a valuable asset, many people are logically unwilling to take time away from work and family life to do a full-time MBA. This is why executive PGDM is important—it provides in-depth, rigorous, customised training for career-focused professionals and can be completed in hybrid mode in as little as 18 months.

Taxila's Executive PGDM entices some of the most thought-provoking, entrepreneurial and ambitious minds in the country — from the Middle level Executives to the Directors of family-run businesses.

Taxila's EPGDM will add value to your resume, company and salary — without putting your career on hold.

One of the most important highlights of Taxila EPGDM is that you can follow your studies without taking a career break and complete the PG Degree program in as little as 18 months.

It may also mean good things for career development in your current company. Executives are likely to take note and be keen to hold the valuable new leadership and management skills you bring to the board room. This will put you in a solid position when it comes to negotiating promotions and salary.

One may position oneself for a great career change — in business, function, or position:

If you have your eye on a major career move, earning an EPGDM could be the most important choice you make.

The knowledge, skills and practical experience you’ll get in the classroom and through case studies, live projects or business simulations will build on your existing professional expertise and help to push your career in a thrilling new direction. The same experiences and new thoughts will also be responsible for entrepreneurs to launch their own business ventures.

Prominently, at this level of study, you will also focus on developing and purifying the skills needed for victory in a leadership role, whatever your industry.

You will improve your professional network with different, fascinating and successful people:

The executives you’ll meet during your course will prove priceless as friends and mentors and even as future co-workers or collaborators. Your classmates are expected to come from all over the country and bring a varied range of expertise and professional experience to the classroom, permitting you to advantage from their exceptional perspective and proficiency. You may find you’re learning alongside a successful tech entrepreneur, a senior scientist, or the General Manager of an international company.

You should be able to put classroom learnings into practice and add value to your company directly.

Being able to apply the learnings from your Executive PGDM to your job immediately is big plus for your employer and makes a b case when trying to get employer to fund your Executive PGDM for your degree.

Core Course

The entire 18 month EPGDM course is delivered through a carefully selected 25 core and elective specialization courses of 3 credits each delivered over first 5 trimesters. The final i.e. 6th trimester will have a project course equal to 6 credits. Each credit is equal to 10 hours of class engagement. The Course will be delivered in a hybrid mode where the students will have a choice to attend their classes online or offline. The usual classes will be held at weekends on Saturday - 06:00 PM to 09:00 PM and on Sunday 10:00 AM to 01:00 PM.

The subjects covered in the core courses are spread over general management and major functional areas, such as Economics, Finance and Accounting, Marketing, Consumer Behaviour, MIS & Operations Management, Organization Behaviour, Human Resources, Research Methods, Statistics & Advanced MS-Excel, Data Analytics, Digital Marketing, Leadership, Strategy etc..

Creating a Solid Foundation

Taxila EPGDM provides students with the same solid foundation of management principles & practices as a two year regular PGDM/MBA, with similar rigour, range of specializations, learning outcomes & placement opportunities.

Faculty at Taxila consists of two Former Vice Chancellors, PhD & Alumni of IIMA and other reputed institutions and practicing CEOs. There are more than 2000 Taxila Alumni working across the globe.

Specializations and Courses

The Elective Specialization courses will be offered in 6 functional areas as follows:-

  • Financial Management
  • Marketing Management
  • Human Relations Management
  • Business Analytics
  • Digital Marketing
  • Operations & Supply Chain

Every student will have an opportunity to specialize in one functional area from the six enlisted above. Every specializaon will have 6 courses together equivalent to 18 credits.
